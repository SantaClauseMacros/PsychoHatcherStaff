Fisch Mode - Auto Fishing

Psycho Hatchers Fisch Mode Guide

This guide provides detailed setup instructions for the Fisch Mode, which is designed for automating the fishing process in Pet Simulator 99.

How to Use Fisch Mode

  1. Start Near Water: Make sure you're close to the water for Fisch Mode to work.
  2. Activate the Rod: Ensure your fishing rod is ready for use by pressing 1.
  3. Hide UI: You need to hide the UI by clicking the camera icon at the top right of the screen.
  4. Press F1: Hit F1 to activate Fisch Mode.

Short Tutorial Version

  • Open Fisch game
  • Start the Mode (but don't hit F1)
  • Walk to water
  • Get rod out by pressing 1
  • Click camera icon
  • Run mode = Press F1

Common Issues

Not near water
Ask: "Are you positioned close to water?"
Fix: Make sure you're standing close to a water source for the mode to work properly.
Fishing rod not activated
Ask: "Did you press 1 to get your fishing rod out?"
Fix: Press 1 to equip your fishing rod before activating the mode.
UI not hidden
Ask: "Did you click the camera icon to hide the UI?"
Fix: Click the camera icon at the top right of the screen to hide the UI before starting.
Mode not activated
Ask: "Did you press F1 to activate the mode?"
Fix: Press F1 after completing all previous steps to activate Fisch Mode.
