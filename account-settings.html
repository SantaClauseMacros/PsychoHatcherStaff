How to Access Account Settings

Having Trouble Finding Settings?

If you're having trouble accessing account settings:

  1. Click the "Account" button in the top right header
  2. If page appears to be bugged, try reloading the page
  3. Make sure you're logged in (you may need to log in again)

Note: If settings still don't appear, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser.

Password Change Request

Submit a request to update your account password

Password change requests are reviewed by Santa for security purposes. Please provide your current password and desired new password.

Password Requirements

At least 8 characters in length
At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
At least one number (0-9)
At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
