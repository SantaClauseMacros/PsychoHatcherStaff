Deep Pool Fishing Mode

Psycho Hatchers Deep Pool Fishing Macro Guide

This guide will walk you through the setup and use of the Psycho Hatcher's Fishing Mode in Pet Sim 99. Follow these steps carefully to ensure everything runs smoothly.

Step-by-Step Setup

  1. Head to the Advanced Fishing Zone: Go to Zone 92, the Advanced Fishing Zone.
  2. Adjust Your Camera View:
    • Set your camera to overhead view.
    • Zoom out as far as the game allows to get the best coverage.
  3. Run the Mode: Once you're in the right position, start the mode.

Optional: Use "Single Window Mode"

If you want only one window to fish while your other accounts remain active, enable "Single Window Mode". This ensures your other accounts continue working on their respective tasks without interruption.

Important Setup Checklist

100% Scaling
Ensure your display settings are set to 100% scaling.
Roblox Not in Full Screen
Run Roblox in windowed mode, not fullscreen.
Level 30 Fishing Mastery
This mode only targets deep pools, which require Level 30 mastery.
Graphics at Level 6 or Higher
The mode needs higher graphics settings for smooth operation.
Leaderboard Open and Opaque
Make sure your leaderboard is open and set to opaque when starting the mode.

Common Issues

Roblox graphics not set to level 6 or higher
Ask: "Are your Roblox graphics set to level 6 or higher?"
Fix: Go to Roblox settings (in-game), then navigate to the Graphics tab. Set the graphics quality to level 6 or higher.
Not in the fishing zone
Ask: "Are you in the correct fishing zone?"
Fix: Ensure you are in the Advanced Fishing Zone (Zone 92) and not in the normal fishing zone.
Camera not in overhead view
Ask: "Is your camera set to overhead view?"
Fix: Adjust your camera to be directly overhead and zoomed out as far as possible.
Fishing mastery too low
Ask: "Is your fishing mastery at level 30 or higher?"
Fix: Level up your fishing mastery to at least level 30 to access deep pools.
Leaderboard not open or not opaque
Ask: "Is your leaderboard open and set to opaque?"
Fix: Open the leaderboard and ensure it's set to opaque before starting the mode.
